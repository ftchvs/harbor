Universal native menu-bar app for Apple silicon and Intel Macs. Harbor ships as a signed, notarized DMG for macOS 14+.

Feature overview

Built for the local dev loop.

Every listening port

See localhost listeners across IPv4 and IPv6 without running lsof or hunting through terminal tabs.

Framework detection

Identify common dev stacks such as Next.js, Vite, Rails, Django, Postgres, and more from the process behind each port.

Live vitals

Track uptime, CPU, memory, and a quick energy badge so noisy servers stand out before they slow your machine down.

One-click actions

Open the app in your browser, jump to the owning terminal, kill one process, or clear the whole local stack with Kill All.

Trust model

Local-first by design.

Non-sandboxed for a reason. Harbor needs to inspect and signal your own local processes to map ports and terminate runaway servers. The app ships outside the Mac App Store as a Developer ID-signed, notarized download.

No telemetry. Harbor does not collect analytics or send process, port, project, or command data to a service. Inspection happens on your Mac.

Verifiable downloads. The release DMG is paired with a SHA256 checksum so you can verify the asset you installed.
